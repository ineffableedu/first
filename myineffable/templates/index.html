WELCOME TO INEFFABLE EDUCATION

INTRODUCTION

Greetings and a warm welcome to the official website of Ineffable Group Of Institution! In today's world, computer literacy and technological savviness have become crucial components of our daily lives. Understanding this need of the hour, IGI has taken up the task of providing computer training programs across India, with an aim to promote computer education for all. We encourage every Indian to join us in this golden mission of achieving computer and skill literacy.

As an Indian non-profit organization working at a national level, IGI is passionate about empowering the underprivileged through computer education and skill development programs. Through our efforts, we firmly believe that education in market-oriented skills can bridge the gap between employment demand and supply, thereby changing lives.

Ineffable Group Of Institution is an autonomous institute specializing in Computer & Skill Education. Committed to excellence, we are registered under The Society & Public Trust Act and certified as an ISO 9001:2015 Computer Educational Organization run by Ineffable Group Of Institution Trust.

Our prime objective is to spread computer & skills literacy throughout India, ensuring that students are self-motivated learners who continuously improve their knowledge base. We serve our purpose by providing affordable technical & skill education for every segment of society - urban or rural - thus contributing towards India's IT and SKILL revolution which is a dream for many.

We realized that some big institutions charged exorbitant fees to provide higher technical education programs lasting over a year. This rendered them out of reach for many middle-class families. Taking this into account, we formulated our mission plan with utmost care by creating "Ineffable Group Of Institution." As per this mission plan set forth by us at Ineffable Group Of Institution all over India; we offer better quality technical education in computers & Skills at affordable prices.
